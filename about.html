About me

Hello, my name is Niamh Brown

I've recently discovered a passion for coding and have decided to make it my mission to become a front-end developer! I have a Mechanical Engineering degree from the University of Sussex and I have been learning to code since June 2021. I am currently on a challenging and fun coding journey with SheCodes Workshops. I’m very proud of the progress I have made so far- from not being able to write one line of code a few months ago, to building my very own portfolio to display my projects! I believe that consistency, determination and a whole lot of self belief will get me to where I dream of being, and would love to share what I learn along the way with other women who are thinking about getting into tech. I love meeting new, like-minded and interesting people… let’s connect on LinkedIn and share ideas and inspiration!
