About me

Hello, my name is Niamh Brown

I've recently discovered a passion for coding and dream of becoming a front-end developer! I have a mechanical engineering degree from the University of Sussex and I have been learning to code since June 2021. I am very proud of the progress I have made so far- from not being able to write one line of code to building my very own portfolio to display my projects! I am currently on a very challenging and fun coding journey with SheCodes Workshops and am very excited and optimistic about where these new coding skills will take me in the future. I believe that consistency, determination and self belief will get me to where I want to be and would like to share what I learn along the way with other women who are thinking about getting into tech- to uplift and inspire and to spread the very important message that WE CAN DO HARD THINGS and nothing is too big for us. I love meeting new, like-minded and interesting people and would love to connect on LinkedIn to share ideas, motivation and inspiration.
